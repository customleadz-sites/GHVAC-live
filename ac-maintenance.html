Why It Matters

What a Yearly Tune-Up
Does for Your Home

In Augusta's climate, your AC runs hard for months straight. Regular maintenance keeps it efficient, reliable, and out of the emergency repair category.

Lower Energy Bills

A dirty, unmaintained system works harder and uses more electricity. A tune-up restores efficiency — often 5–15% savings on your monthly bill.

Prevent Costly Breakdowns

Most AC failures are preventable. We catch worn parts, low refrigerant, and failing components before they turn into emergency calls in August heat.

Extend System Life

Well-maintained AC systems last 15+ years. Skipping maintenance is the #1 reason systems fail before their time — costing you an early replacement.

Better Air Quality

Dirty coils and filters circulate dust, allergens, and mold spores through your home. A tune-up includes a thorough cleaning that improves the air your family breathes.

Stay Cool All Summer

A properly tuned AC reaches your thermostat setting faster, holds the temperature better, and handles Georgia heat the way it's supposed to.

Protect Your Warranty

Many manufacturer warranties require annual maintenance to stay valid. We provide documentation of the service so you're covered if you ever need to make a claim.

What We Do

What's Included in Every
Giesbrecht AC Tune-Up

A thorough inspection and maintenance — not just a filter change. Here's exactly what we check.

Inspect & Clean Evaporator Coil

Dirty coils reduce efficiency dramatically. We clean them thoroughly to restore cooling performance.

Inspect & Clean Condenser Coil

The outdoor unit collects dirt and debris. A clean condenser runs cooler and more efficiently.

Check Refrigerant Levels

Low refrigerant is a common cause of warm air and high bills. We test and report on system charge.

Test Capacitors & Contactors

These small parts fail without warning. We test them so you know their condition before they take out your system.

Check & Clear Drain Line

Clogged drain lines cause water damage and mold. We flush and clear the drain to prevent problems.

Inspect Electrical Connections

Loose or corroded connections are a fire hazard and cause intermittent failures. We inspect and tighten all connections.

Test Thermostat Accuracy

A miscalibrated thermostat means your system runs more than it needs to. We verify it's reading and responding correctly.

Full System Performance Report

We document everything we find and give you a clear report — including any recommendations and what's urgent vs. what can wait.
