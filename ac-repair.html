Signs Your AC Needs Repair Now

Don't wait until your system quits completely. These warning signs mean it's time to call.

Warm or Weak Airflow

Your AC is running but the air coming out isn't cold — or barely any air at all. A failing compressor or low refrigerant is usually the cause.

Sky-High Energy Bills

A struggling AC works overtime to reach your thermostat setting, driving up your bill. We find the inefficiency and fix it for good.

Strange Noises or Smells

Banging, grinding, or musty smells aren't normal. These are signs something is wrong internally and needs immediate attention.

High Indoor Humidity

Your AC removes humidity as it cools. If your home feels sticky and muggy, your system isn't doing its job — and we can fix that.

Frequent Cycling On & Off

Short-cycling — when your AC turns on and off too often — wears out parts fast. This needs to be diagnosed before the system quits entirely.

AC Stopped Working Completely

Full system failure in Augusta heat is an emergency. Call us now — we prioritize same-day dispatch for AC breakdowns.
