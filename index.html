Most energy experts recommend 78°F when you're home and 82–85°F when away. Every degree you raise saves roughly 3% on cooling costs. If your system struggles to reach your set temperature, that's a sign it needs service.

High bills usually point to a dirty or aging system, leaky ductwork, or a refrigerant issue. Our energy audit service pinpoints exactly where you're losing efficiency and gives you a clear plan to fix it.

The ideal indoor humidity range is 45–55%. Below 40% causes dry air and wood damage. Above 60% encourages mold and dust mites. We install whole-home humidity control systems to keep you in that range year-round.
